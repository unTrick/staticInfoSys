Templates 3 Strike-Rule
Daily Process
DAY 1

Set the ticket to “Pending” then contact the user by sending 1st Strike email, please use the
template below:

Subject: [(Ticket Number – Summary of the issue (eg. Unable to login to Outlook)]
Dear [(User / Customer)],

We are contacting you in reference to [Ticket Number] regarding [Issue].
We may not be able to proceed as the above ticket needs clarification from your side. To resolve your
issue or concern, or to process your request please:

<Resolution/Needs more information/Needs troubleshooting>

For further assistance please call the MVW Service Desk at 833-435-7689, we are open 24/7, we would
be more than happy to assist you.

Regards,
SD Name
MVW Service Desk

Day 2

If the user did not respond to your 1st email, then send them the 2nd Strike email, please use
the template below:

Subject: [(Ticket Number – Summary of the issue (eg. Unable to login to Outlook)]
Dear [(User / Customer)],

We are contacting you in reference to [Ticket Number] regarding [Issue]. This is our 2nd attempt to
gather more information on your incident.
We may not be able to proceed as the above ticket needs clarification from your side. To resolve your
issue or concern, or to process your request please:

<Resolution/Needs more information/Needs troubleshooting>
For further assistance please call the MVW Service Desk at 833-435-7689, we are open 24/7, we would
be more than happy to assist you.

Regards,
SD Name
MVW Service Desk

Day 3

If user did not respond to your 1st and 2nd strike email, send them the 3rd strike email:

Subject: [(Ticket Number – Summary of the issue (eg. Unable to login to Outlook)]
Dear [(User / Customer)],
We are contacting you in reference to [Ticket Number] regarding [Issue].
We may not be able to proceed as the above ticket needs clarification from your side. To resolve your
issue or concern, or to process your request please:

<Resolution/Needs more information/Needs troubleshooting>
We have made several attempts to contact you regarding this ticket but have not received a response.
Unfortunately, we must close this ticket for no response. If you still need further assistance, you can
always open a new ticket, please use the following options:
Call the IT Service Desk at 833-435-7689
Dash-HR & IT Chat Support.
Report an issue or submit a request via Service Catalog or pITstop.

Regards,
SD Name
MVW Service Desk

Note

If the user did not respond within 2 hours, you may resolve the ticket.
