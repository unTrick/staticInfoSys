Squirrel Call and Escalation Process

Office hours:

 1. Accept call, ask for all required information
 2. Perform “basic” troubleshooting as per KB depending on the issue
 3. Unless it's something super simple that you know you can fix and you don't need to forward it
   anywhere, you just hand it over to the MVW team. Message MVW Team in our Service Desk
   Daily Escalations Teams Channel.
 • Make sure your message is tagged as IMPORTANT (Click the edit for edit text, click the
   exclamation point to tag it as Important)
 • Make sure to message them using the “Approved” Standard Squirrel Escalation Template below:

Name:

Site / Location:

Property/Restaurant Name

Contact Details: via Phone# or Teams:

Actual Issue:

Affected Terminal Name (If applicable):

Impact:

Cherwell Ticket#:

 • Make sure to wait for MVW to respond to your message
 4. Once you received a response from our MVW Team, inform and assure the user that someone
   will contact them to take care of the issue (DO NOT provide estimated time)
 5. If you don't get any answer within 5 minutes, you can call MVW Escalations Line (It's already in
   place, they already have an active rotation and It is manned 24/7)
 6. The MVW team will take over the ticket, follows process and then if they have to escalate to the
   MODS, they will go ahead and escalate it.

Off Hours and Weekends :

 1. Accept call, ask for all required information
 2. Perform “basic” troubleshooting AS PER KB ARTICLE(S), depending on the issue
 3. Unless it's something super simple that you know you can fix and you don't need to forward it
   anywhere, you just hand it over to our Squirrel EXPERTS(which will be announced soon). If
   there’s no available Squirrel EXPERTS, then escalate it to our MOD. (We will create a Teams
   channel for that)

 • Make sure your message is tagged as IMPORTANT (Click the edit for edit text, click the
   exclamation point to tag it as Important)
 • Make sure to message them using the “Approved” Standard Squirrel Escalation Template below:

 • Make sure to wait for the Experts/MODs to respond to your message
 4. Once you received a response from our Experts/MOD Team, inform and assure the user
   that someone will contact them to take care of the issue (DO NOT provide estimated time)
 5. If you don't get any answer within 5 minutes, you can call the MOD Escalations Line
 6. The Squirrel Experts/MOD will take over the ticket.
