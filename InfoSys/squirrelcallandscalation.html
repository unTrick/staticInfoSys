 • Make sure to wait for MVW to respond to your message
 4. Once you received a response from our MVW Team, inform and assure the user that someone will contact them to take care of the issue
  (DO NOT provide estimated time)
 5. If you don't get any answer within 5 minutes, you can call MVW Escalations Line (It's already in place, they already have an active rotation and It is manned 24/7)
 6. The MVW team will take over the ticket, follows process and then if they have to escalate to the MODS, they will go ahead and escalate it.
