Account Issues Expired Account
1. Look up the user’s EID on Active Directory
2. Check the properties and go to “Account Tab”
3. Under Account Expires, you should be able to see the expiration date of the user’s account
4. If its due already, like the picture below, have them reach out to their manager to request an account reactivation on PiTstop
Note

Service Catalog for account re-enable/re-activation is “EID Services”.
