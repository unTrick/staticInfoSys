Application Issues Universe or Documentum Not Connecting
Universe Session Not Connecting – “Attempting to Connect to Host” at the bottom OR Documentum RDP Sessions fail.
This error is caused by the PC not be allowed through the Firewall. Below are the items needed to get through the firewall

 • PC has up to date Group Policy
 • User has AD Group
 • PC has AD Group
 • User is on VPN or Ethernet corporate network connection
 • Host Access is properly configured (Universe Only)

 Troubleshooting Steps:

 1. To update the Group Policy... If the user is on MVW Network open Command Prompt and type: gpupdate /force

 2. Make sure they are not trying to access Universe and/or Documentum via MVW Global Wireless or Public Wifi without VPN Connection

 3. Make Sure they are logging in with their own EID (not IMG or generic account) IMG accounts do not have access to Universe.

 4. EID has GRP-Universe-Users group for Universe, or GRP-Documentum-Users for Documentum in
  Active Directory – Refer to Cherwell Service Catalog for Universe Access or Documentum Access -
  DO NOT ADD AD GROUPS TO EID - refer to Cherwell pIT Stop

 5. User is entering EID all in lower case

 6. User EID is not locked out.

 7. Make sure the PC is in the GRP-CDE-Computers AD group for Universe or GRP-Documentum-
  Computers for Documentum - Contact MOD to add the AD group(s) to the PC

 8. Make sure the PC is in the correct PCI OU – Forward ticket to Central Ops to move PC to PCI OU PC
  must have the correct AD group and BE in the PCI OU to be able to connect.

 Wait 15 – 30 minutes for the updated permissions to be loaded for the PC, it can take 15 minutes.
